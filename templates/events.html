Join the Fight Against Plastic Pollution

Plastic pollution is one of the most pressing environmental challenges we face today. By organizing cleanup events, you can:

  • Protect marine life and ecosystems
  • Reduce pollution in our communities
  • Raise awareness about waste management
  • Create lasting positive change

Organizer Requirements:

  • Strong coordination and leadership skills
  • Knowledge of proper waste segregation
  • Ability to manage volunteers
  • Commitment to environmental safety
